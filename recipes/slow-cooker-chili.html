Slow Cooker Chili

Description

Who doesn't love a nice, hearty bowl of chili during a chilly (get it?) fall or winter day? Lucky for you, we have a simple recipe to create this delicious meal, and all you need are a few ingredients and a slow cooker.

This is one of my favorite meals to prepare because it's delicious, easy to make, and gives you leftovers for a few nights during the week. It should be noted that ground turkey or chicken can be substituted in for the ground beef that is called out in the recipe. If you're on a plant-based diet, you could even throw in a meat substitute like Impossible burger meat or something similar.
