Slow Cooker Chili

Description

Who doesn't love a nice, hearty bowl of chili during a chilly (get it?) fall or winter day? Lucky for you, we have a simple recipe to create this delicious meal, and all you need are a few ingredients and a slow cooker.

This is one of my favorite meals to prepare because it's delicious, easy to make, and gives you leftovers for a few nights during the week. It should be noted that ground turkey or chicken can be substituted in for the ground beef that is called out in the recipe. If you're on a plant-based diet, you could even throw in a meat substitute like Impossible burger meat or something similar.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Jalapeno Peppers, chopped (optional)
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce (8 oz cans)
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes (14.5 oz cans)
  • 2 tbsp of chili powder
  • 2.5 tsp of cumin
  • 2 tsp of paprika
  • 0.5 tsp of coriander
  • Shredded cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop onion, garlic, and jalapeno peppers.
  2. Brown ground beef in a sauce pan, drain fat, and add to slow cooker.
  3. Saute onions in sauce pan, adding garlic for 30 seconds. Add onions, garlic, and jalapeno peppers to slow cooker.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and coriander to slow cooker. Stir ingredients so they are blended together evenly.
  5. Cook chili on low for 6 hours.
  6. Optional Step: Serve chili and add shredded cheese to taste
